INSPECTION LOGIC:
- Tracks CUMULATIVE RECEIVED quantity from Finishing
- Inspects 10% SAMPLE of received quantity
- Auto-suggests 10% for accuracy

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  THREE FIELDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Quantity Received TODAY from Finishing (Required)
   - Actual quantity received TODAY
   - Tracks cumulative received
   - Example: Day 1 = 500, Day 2 = 300, Total = 800

2. Quantity Inspected TODAY - 10% Sample (Required)
   - 10% sample inspected TODAY
   - Auto-suggests 10% of received
   - Example: Received 500 → Suggested 50 (10%)

3. Progress Display
   - Total Received: Cumulative from Finishing
   - Total Inspected: Cumulative 10% samples
   - Remaining: Yet to receive/inspect
