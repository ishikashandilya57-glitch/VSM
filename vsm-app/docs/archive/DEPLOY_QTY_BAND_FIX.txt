🔢 QUANTITY BAND LOGIC
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q2: ≤ 3000 pcs  → Actual data

WHY MORE TIME FOR HIGHER QUANTITIES?
- Capacity constraints (limited machines/workers)
- More setup changes
- Quality control takes longer
- Material handling increases
